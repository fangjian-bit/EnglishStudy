# 02 · 叙述与故事

> 短叙事句：经历、回忆、事件顺序。  
> 返回 → [索引.md](./索引.md)

---

### 1
**EN:** Yesterday I woke up early and went for a run in the park.
**CN:** 昨天我起得很早，去公园跑了个步。

### 2
**EN:** The sky was gray, but the air felt fresh and cool.
**CN:** 天空灰蒙蒙的，但空气清新凉爽。

### 3
**EN:** After breakfast, I packed my bag and left for the office.
**CN:** 早饭后，我收拾好包去上班了。

### 4
**EN:** On the way, I ran into an old friend I hadn't seen in years.
**CN:** 路上我偶遇了一位多年未见的老朋友。

### 5
**EN:** We stopped at a café and talked for almost an hour.
**CN:** 我们在一家咖啡馆停下来，聊了近一个小时。

### 6
**EN:** She told me she had moved to another city for work.
**CN:** 她告诉我，她为了工作搬到了另一座城市。

### 7
**EN:** I felt happy to hear her news, but also a little sad.
**CN:** 听到她的消息我很高兴，但也有点难过。

### 8
**EN:** When I was a child, my family lived in a small town by the river.
**CN:** 小时候，我家住在河边的一座小镇上。

### 9
**EN:** Every summer, we swam in the river and caught fireflies at night.
**CN:** 每年夏天，我们在河里游泳，晚上捉萤火虫。

### 10
**EN:** Those days seemed long and peaceful, as if time would never end.
**CN:** 那些日子漫长而安宁，仿佛时间永远不会结束。

### 11
**EN:** One rainy afternoon, the power went out in our building.
**CN:** 一个下雨的下午，我们楼里停电了。

### 12
**EN:** My mother lit candles, and we read stories by the window.
**CN:** 妈妈点上蜡烛，我们在窗边读故事。

### 13
**EN:** I still remember the sound of rain on the glass.
**CN:** 我仍记得雨点打在玻璃上的声音。

### 14
**EN:** Last weekend, we drove to the mountains for a short trip.
**CN:** 上周末，我们开车去山里短途旅行。

### 15
**EN:** The road was narrow and winding, but the views were amazing.
**CN:** 路又窄又弯，但风景美极了。

### 16
**EN:** We reached the top just before sunset.
**CN:** 我们在日落前刚好到了山顶。

### 17
**EN:** The whole valley turned gold in the last light of the day.
**CN:** 整片山谷在夕阳最后的余晖里变成了金色。

### 18
**EN:** On my first day at the new school, I didn't know anyone.
**CN:** 在新学校的第一天，我谁也不认识。

### 19
**EN:** A girl in my class smiled and asked me to sit with her.
**CN:** 班里一个女孩笑着让我和她坐在一起。

### 20
**EN:** That small act made me feel welcome.
**CN:** 那个小小的举动让我觉得自己被接纳了。

### 21
**EN:** The exam was harder than I expected.
**CN:** 考试比我想象的难。

### 22
**EN:** I stayed up late reviewing my notes, but I still felt nervous.
**CN:** 我熬夜复习笔记，但仍然很紧张。

### 23
**EN:** When the results came out, I was surprised—I had passed with a high score.
**CN:** 成绩出来时我很惊讶——我高分通过了。

### 24
**EN:** My teacher said I had improved a lot over the semester.
**CN:** 老师说这学期我进步很大。

### 25
**EN:** The dog ran into the street, and a car stopped just in time.
**CN:** 狗冲到了马路上，一辆车及时停了下来。

### 26
**EN:** A stranger picked it up and looked for the owner.
**CN:** 一位路人把它抱起来，寻找主人。

### 27
**EN:** Half an hour later, a worried boy came running down the block.
**CN:** 半小时后，一个焦急的男孩沿着街区跑过来。

### 28
**EN:** The reunion was brief, but everyone in the crowd smiled.
**CN:** 重逢很短暂，但围观的人都笑了。

### 29
**EN:** I lost my wallet on the train and thought it was gone for good.
**CN:** 我在火车上丢了钱包，以为找不回来了。

### 30
**EN:** The next day, the station office called to say someone had found it.
**CN:** 第二天，车站办公室打来电话，说有人捡到了。

### 31
**EN:** Inside everything was still there, even the photo of my parents.
**CN:** 里面的东西都在，连我父母的照片也在。

### 32
**EN:** I wrote a thank-you note and left it at the lost-and-found desk.
**CN:** 我写了一张感谢便条，放在失物招领处。

### 33
**EN:** As the year ended, I looked back at both my failures and my wins.
**CN:** 年末时，我回顾了这一年的失败与收获。

### 34
**EN:** Not everything went as planned, but I learned to keep trying.
**CN:** 并非事事如愿，但我学会了坚持。

### 35
**EN:** On New Year's Eve, I made a quiet promise to myself to read more and worry less.
**CN:** 除夕夜，我默默向自己承诺：多读书，少焦虑。

### 36
**EN:** When I moved to the city, everything felt strange at first.
**CN:** 刚搬到城里时，一切都觉得很陌生。

### 37
**EN:** I got lost on my way to the supermarket more than once.
**CN:** 我去超市的路上不止一次迷路。

### 38
**EN:** Slowly, I learned the streets and found a favorite noodle shop.
**CN:** 慢慢地，我熟悉了街道，还找到一家喜欢的面馆。

### 39
**EN:** The owner remembered my order after only a few visits.
**CN:** 老板来过几次就记住了我常点的菜。

### 40
**EN:** One winter morning, I slipped on ice and fell in front of a crowd.
**CN:** 一个冬天的早晨，我在冰上滑倒，摔在人群面前。

### 41
**EN:** My face turned red, but a man helped me up and made sure I was okay.
**CN:** 我脸都红了，但一位男士扶我起来，还确认我没事。

### 42
**EN:** I was embarrassed, yet I felt grateful for his kindness.
**CN:** 我很尴尬，但也感激他的善意。

### 43
**EN:** My grandmother taught me to cook when I was twelve.
**CN:** 我十二岁时，奶奶教我做饭。

### 44
**EN:** She never used measuring cups—she just knew by feel.
**CN:** 她从来不用量杯——全凭手感。

### 45
**EN:** I still use her recipe for tomato soup on cold days.
**CN:** 天冷的日子，我仍按她的方子做番茄汤。

### 46
**EN:** The smell of garlic and herbs fills the kitchen and brings back old memories.
**CN:** 大蒜和香草的气味充满厨房，勾起旧时回忆。

### 47
**EN:** During the holiday, our whole family gathered at my uncle's house.
**CN:** 假期里，全家聚在叔叔家。

### 48
**EN:** Children ran around the yard while adults talked and laughed in the living room.
**CN:** 孩子在院子里跑闹，大人在客厅说笑。

### 49
**EN:** We ate too much, played cards, and stayed up until midnight.
**CN:** 我们吃得太饱，打牌，一直熬到半夜。

### 50
**EN:** On the drive home, I felt tired but deeply content.
**CN:** 开车回家的路上，我很累，但心里特别满足。

### 51
**EN:** I once volunteered at a community center on weekends.
**CN:** 我曾周末在社区中心做志愿者。

### 52
**EN:** I helped kids with homework and organized simple art activities.
**CN:** 我帮孩子们辅导作业，组织简单的手工活动。

### 53
**EN:** One boy was shy at first and barely spoke.
**CN:** 有个男孩起初很害羞，几乎不说话。

### 54
**EN:** By the end of the month, he was showing me his drawings with a big smile.
**CN:** 到了月底，他笑着给我看自己的画。

### 55
**EN:** That experience reminded me how small acts of care can change someone's week.
**CN:** 那段经历让我明白，小小的关心也能改变别人的一周。
