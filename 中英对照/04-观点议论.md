# 04 · 观点议论

> 观点、理由、比较、建议与议论。

### 1
**EN:** In my opinion, learning a language takes patience more than talent.
**CN:** 在我看来，学语言更需要耐心，而不是天赋。

### 2
**EN:** I believe daily practice matters more than occasional long sessions.
**CN:** 我认为每天练一点，比偶尔长时间突击更重要。

### 3
**EN:** Some people prefer classroom study; others learn better on their own.
**CN:** 有人喜欢课堂学习，有人自学效果更好。

### 4
**EN:** Both methods can work if you stay consistent.
**CN:** 只要坚持，两种方法都可以奏效。

### 5
**EN:** I think reading widely is one of the best ways to expand vocabulary.
**CN:** 我认为广泛阅读是扩大词汇量最好的方式之一。

### 6
**EN:** When you see words in context, you remember them more easily.
**CN:** 在语境中看到单词，会更容易记住。

### 7
**EN:** Memorizing lists alone is helpful, but it is not enough.
**CN:** 只背单词表有帮助，但还不够。

### 8
**EN:** I would argue that speaking practice should start early.
**CN:** 我认为口语练习应该尽早开始。

### 9
**EN:** Many learners wait until they feel "ready," but confidence comes from trying.
**CN:** 很多人等到觉得"准备好了"再说，但信心来自尝试。

### 10
**EN:** Making mistakes is part of learning, not a sign of failure.
**CN:** 犯错是学习的一部分，不是失败的标志。

### 11
**EN:** Compared with ten years ago, online resources are far more accessible.
**CN:** 与十年前相比，在线资源容易获取得多。

### 12
**EN:** However, too many choices can also be confusing.
**CN:** 然而，选择太多也会让人困惑。

### 13
**EN:** It is better to pick a few good tools and use them regularly.
**CN:** 不如选几个好工具，坚持使用。

### 14
**EN:** I prefer podcasts because I can listen while commuting.
**CN:** 我更喜欢播客，因为通勤时也能听。

### 15
**EN:** On the other hand, videos are useful when you need visual explanations.
**CN:** 另一方面，需要图像讲解时，视频很有用。

### 16
**EN:** Working from home saves travel time, but it can feel isolating.
**CN:** 居家办公省通勤时间，但可能让人感到孤立。

### 17
**EN:** A mix of office days and remote days may offer the best balance.
**CN:** 办公室与远程相结合，或许是最佳平衡。

### 18
**EN:** I strongly support policies that protect the environment.
**CN:** 我强烈支持保护环境的政策。

### 19
**EN:** Climate change affects everyone, so everyone should take action.
**CN:** 气候变化影响每个人，因此每个人都应行动。

### 20
**EN:** Individual choices matter, but governments and companies must lead as well.
**CN:** 个人选择重要，但政府和企业也必须带头。

### 21
**EN:** Some say technology makes us less social; I partly agree.
**CN:** 有人说科技让我们更少社交；我部分同意。

### 22
**EN:** Phones can distract us, yet they also help us stay connected across distance.
**CN:** 手机会分散注意力，但也帮我们跨越距离保持联系。

### 23
**EN:** The key is to use technology with intention, not on autopilot.
**CN:** 关键在于有意使用科技，而不是无意识刷屏。

### 24
**EN:** I recommend setting fixed times to check messages.
**CN:** 我建议固定时间查看消息。

### 25
**EN:** If possible, keep phones out of the bedroom at night.
**CN:** 如果可能，晚上不要把手机放在卧室。

### 26
**EN:** Education should teach students how to think, not just what to memorize.
**CN:** 教育应教学生如何思考，而不只是记什么。

### 27
**EN:** Critical thinking helps people evaluate information in the digital age.
**CN:** 批判性思维帮助人们在数字时代评估信息。

### 28
**EN:** Schools should also teach media literacy and basic research skills.
**CN:** 学校还应教授媒介素养和基本研究技能。

### 29
**EN:** Although exams are necessary, they should not be the only measure of success.
**CN:** 考试虽有必要，但不应是衡量成功的唯一标准。

### 30
**EN:** Creativity, teamwork, and communication deserve equal attention.
**CN:** 创造力、团队合作和沟通能力同样值得重视。

### 31
**EN:** From my experience, the hardest part of learning English is speaking fluently.
**CN:** 以我的经验，学英语最难的是说得流利。

### 32
**EN:** Fluency comes from repetition, not from knowing every grammar rule.
**CN:** 流利来自重复练习，而非掌握每一条语法规则。

### 33
**EN:** I suggest finding a language partner or joining a small conversation group.
**CN:** 我建议找语伴或加入小型口语小组。

### 34
**EN:** Even fifteen minutes a day can build confidence over months.
**CN:** 即使每天十五分钟，几个月后也能建立信心。

### 35
**EN:** In conclusion, progress is slow at first, but it compounds if you do not give up.
**CN:** 总之，起初进步很慢，但只要不放弃，效果会累积起来。

### 36
**EN:** I disagree with the idea that age alone decides whether someone can learn well.
**CN:** 我不同意单凭年龄就能决定一个人学得好不好。

### 37
**EN:** Motivation and method matter just as much as natural ability.
**CN:** 动力和方法与自然能力同样重要。

### 38
**EN:** To be fair, younger learners often pick up pronunciation faster.
**CN:** 公平地说，年轻学习者往往发音掌握得更快。

### 39
**EN:** Still, adults can compensate with discipline and clear goals.
**CN:** 但成年人可以用自律和明确目标来弥补。

### 40
**EN:** I agree that travel broadens the mind, but not everyone can afford it.
**CN:** 我同意旅行能开阔眼界，但并非人人负担得起。

### 41
**EN:** Reading books and watching documentaries can offer a similar benefit at lower cost.
**CN:** 读书和看纪录片能以更低成本获得类似收获。

### 42
**EN:** That said, meeting people from other cultures in person is hard to replace.
**CN:** 话虽如此，当面接触不同文化的人仍难以替代。

### 43
**EN:** In my view, cities should invest more in public parks and bike lanes.
**CN:** 我认为城市应加大投入建设公园和自行车道。

### 44
**EN:** Green spaces improve mental health and encourage outdoor activity.
**CN:** 绿地改善心理健康，鼓励户外活动。

### 45
**EN:** Critics argue it is too expensive, yet the long-term savings in healthcare can be significant.
**CN:** 批评者认为花费太高，但长期医疗开支的节省可能很可观。

**EN:** I would rather walk or take the bus than sit in traffic every day.
**CN:** 我宁愿走路或坐公交，也不愿天天堵在路上。

### 47
**EN:** Convenience matters, but so does quality of life.
**CN:** 便利重要，生活质量同样重要。

### 48
**EN:** Some people think social media is harmless entertainment; I am not so sure.
**CN:** 有人认为社交媒体只是无害的娱乐；我不太确定。

### 49
**EN:** Constant comparison can harm self-esteem, especially among teenagers.
**CN:** 不断攀比会损害自尊，青少年尤其如此。

### 50
**EN:** Platforms should do more to protect users, but individuals must set limits too.
**CN:** 平台应加强保护，但个人也要设定界限。

### 51
**EN:** I partly agree with your point about rising prices.
**CN:** 关于物价上涨，我部分同意你的看法。

### 52
**EN:** Inflation affects groceries and rent, which hurts low-income families most.
**CN:** 通胀影响食品杂货和房租，对低收入家庭打击最大。

**EN:** However, blaming a single cause oversimplifies a complex problem.
**CN:** 然而，归咎于单一原因会把复杂问题过于简单化。

**EN:** What we need is honest discussion and practical solutions, not slogans.
**CN:** 我们需要坦诚讨论和可行方案，而不是口号。

**EN:** All things considered, I remain optimistic that small, steady efforts can lead to real change.
**CN:** 综合考虑，我仍乐观地认为，持续的小努力能带来真正的改变。
