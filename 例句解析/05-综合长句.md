# 05 · 综合长句 · 例句解析

## 1

**英文：** The tall man who lives next door left early yesterday.  
**中文：** 住在隔壁的那个高个子男人昨天很早就走了。

| 成分 | 词 |
|------|-----|
| 主语 | The tall man who lives next door |
| 定语 | tall / who lives next door |
| 谓语 | left |
| 状语 | early / yesterday |
| 结构 | **定语 + 定语从句 + 主谓 + 时间/方式状语** |

**拆解顺序：** 先找主干「男人走了」→ 再挂 tall、who…、early、yesterday。

## 2

**英文：** I think that the book on the desk is very interesting.  
**中文：** 我觉得桌上那本书非常有趣。

| 成分 | 词 |
|------|-----|
| 主语 | I |
| 谓语 | think |
| 宾语从句 | that the book on the desk is very interesting |
| 从句主语 | the book on the desk |
| 从句定语 | on the desk |
| 从句谓语 | is |
| 从句表语 | interesting |
| 从句状语 | very |
| 结构 | **主谓 + 宾语从句（内含定语+主系表）** |

## 3

**英文：** She asked me to help her finish the work before Friday.  
**中文：** 她请我在周五前帮她完成工作。

| 成分 | 词 |
|------|-----|
| 主语 | She |
| 谓语 | asked |
| 宾语 | me |
| 宾补 | to help her finish the work before Friday |
| 结构 | **ask + 宾语 + to do 宾补（嵌套 help sb do）** |

**拆解：** asked me **to help** → 框 A；help her **finish** → help 后接原形 finish。

## 5

**英文：** The old house near the river that my grandfather built needs to be repaired soon.  
**中文：** 河边那栋我祖父建的老房子需要尽快修缮。

| 成分 | 词 |
|------|-----|
| 主语 | The old house near the river that my grandfather built |
| 定语 | old / near the river / that my grandfather built |
| 谓语 | needs |
| 宾语 | to be repaired |
| 状语 | soon |
| 结构 | **多重定语 + 主谓 + 不定式宾语 + 状语** |

**拆解：** 三个定语叠在 house 上——old、near the river、that…built。

## 6

**英文：** I saw the boy who was running quickly toward the school gate.  
**中文：** 我看见那个正飞快地朝校门跑去的男孩。

| 成分 | 词 |
|------|-----|
| 主语 | I |
| 谓语 | saw |
| 宾语 | the boy who was running quickly toward the school gate |
| 定语从句 | who was running quickly toward the school gate |
| 结构 | **主谓宾 + 定语从句（内含状语）** |

**从句内：** quickly = 方式状语；toward the school gate = 方向状语。

## 7

**英文：** What surprised everyone was that he passed the exam without studying.  
**中文：** 让所有人惊讶的是，他没复习就通过了考试。

| 成分 | 词 |
|------|-----|
| 主语从句 | What surprised everyone |
| 谓语 | was |
| 表语从句 | that he passed the exam without studying |
| 结构 | **主语从句 + 主系 + 表语从句** |

**拆解：** 整句主干 = X was Y；X 和 Y 都是整句从句。

## 15

**英文：** The fact that he won the competition surprised everyone who knew him well.  
**中文：** 他赢得比赛这件事让所有熟悉他的人都很惊讶。

| 成分 | 词 |
|------|-----|
| 主语 | The fact that he won the competition |
| 同位语从句 | that he won the competition（解释 fact） |
| 谓语 | surprised |
| 宾语 | everyone who knew him well |
| 定语从句 | who knew him well |
| 结构 | **同位语从句 + 主谓宾 + 定语从句** |

**对比：** 同位语从句 **解释** fact 的内容；定语从句 **限定** everyone。

## 34

**英文：** The old photo hanging on the wall, which was taken on the day we graduated, reminds me of the friends I haven't seen for years.  
**中文：** 挂在墙上、拍于我们毕业那天的那张旧照片，让我想起多年未见的朋友们。

| 成分 | 词 |
|------|-----|
| 主语 | The old photo hanging on the wall, which was taken on the day we graduated |
| 定语 | hanging on the wall |
| 非限制性定语从句 | which was taken on the day we graduated |
| 定语从句 | we graduated（修饰 day） |
| 谓语 | reminds |
| 宾语 | me |
| 宾补 | of the friends I haven't seen for years |
| 定语从句 | I haven't seen for years |
| 结构 | **分词定语 + 非限制性定语从句 + remind sb of + 定语从句** |

**拆解：** photo 哪个？→ hanging… + which was taken…；day 哪天？→ we graduated；friends 哪些？→ I haven't seen…

## 35

**英文：** So difficult was the exam that even the students who had prepared carefully found it hard to answer the questions based on the material the teacher had barely covered in class.  
**中文：** 考试太难了，连认真准备的学生都觉得很难回答那些基于老师课上几乎没讲过的材料的题目。

| 成分 | 词 |
|------|-----|
| 表语 | So difficult |
| 主语 | the exam |
| 谓语 | was（倒装） |
| 结果从句 | that even the students who… found it hard to answer… |
| 定语从句 | who had prepared carefully / the teacher had barely covered in class |
| 结构 | **So…that 倒装 + 定语从句 + find it + 宾补 + 不定式 + 嵌套定语** |

**拆解：** So difficult **was** the exam → 倒装；students 哪些？→ who had prepared…；questions 基于什么？→ material **（that）** teacher had covered…
