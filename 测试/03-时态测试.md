# 03 · 时态测试

> 根据中文意思或语境，选择正确的英文时态填空。  
> 理论 → [../底层笔记/底层时态.md](../底层笔记/底层时态.md)

**说明：** 从括号内选择或写出正确形式。每题只选一个最佳答案。

## 题目

1. 我每天都喝咖啡。  
   I ______ (drink / am drinking / drank) coffee every morning.

2. 此刻她正在写报告。  
   She ______ (writes / is writing / has written) a report right now.

3. 他昨天去了北京。  
   He ______ (goes / went / has gone) to Beijing yesterday.

4. 我已经做完作业了。  
   I ______ (finish / finished / have finished) my homework.

5. 明天下午三点我会在开会。  
   I ______ (will meet / will be meeting / meet) at 3 pm tomorrow.

6. 当我到达时，电影已经开始了。  
   The movie ______ (started / had started / has started) when I arrived.

7. 这学期以来，她一直在学法语。  
   She ______ (studies / has been studying / studied) French since this term began.

8. 1990 年他出生在这个城市。  
   He ______ (is born / was born / has been born) in this city in 1990.

9. 到明年这个时候，我们将在这里住满十年。  
   By this time next year, we ______ (live / will have lived / will be living) here for ten years.

10. 那时孩子们正在操场上玩。  
    The children ______ (played / were playing / have played) on the playground at that time.

11. 水在 100°C 沸腾。  
    Water ______ (boils / is boiling / boiled) at 100°C.

12. 你以前见过他吗？  
    ______ you ever ______ (see / saw / seen) him before?

13. 我下周会给你打电话。  
    I ______ (call / will call / am calling) you next week.

14. 他离开前已经写完了信。  
    He ______ (wrote / had written / has written) the letter before he left.

15. Look! The bus ______ (comes / is coming / came).

16. 我在这家公司工作了五年（现在还在这）。  
    I ______ (work / have worked / worked) for this company for five years.

17. 昨晚八点他在看电视。  
    He ______ (watched / was watching / has watched) TV at 8 pm last night.

18. 到周五之前，项目会完成吗？  
    ______ the project ______ (finish / be finished / have been finished) by Friday?

19. 她通常步行上班，但今天她乘公交。  
    She usually walks to work, but today she ______ (takes / is taking / has taken) the bus.

20. 自从搬家后，我们还没见过他们。  
    We ______ (don't see / didn't see / haven't seen) them since we moved.

21. 2030 年，这座城市将拥有更多绿色空间。  
    The city ______ (has / will have / will be having) more green spaces by 2030.

22. 他 said he ______ (will come / would come / comes) the next day.

23. 火车五点出发，我们最好快点。  
    The train ______ (leaves / is leaving / has left) at five; we'd better hurry.

24. 她整个上午都在等电话。  
    She ______ (waits / has waited / has been waiting) for a call all morning.

25. 去年这个时候，我正在准备考试。  
    This time last year, I ______ (prepare / was preparing / have prepared) for the exam.

26. 到会议结束时，他们已经讨论了三个小时。  
    By the end of the meeting, they ______ (discuss / had discussed / have discussed) for three hours.

27. 如果你继续这样练习，你会进步的。  
    If you keep practicing like this, you ______ (improve / will improve / improved).

28. 我刚洗完碗。  
    I ______ (just wash / just washed / have just washed) the dishes.

29. 那时他已经在这家公司工作十年了。  
    He ______ (worked / had worked / has worked) for the company for ten years by then.

30. 别担心，我会帮你。  
    Don't worry. I ______ (help / will help / am helping) you.

31. 从上周起，雨就一直在下。  
    It ______ (rains / has rained / has been raining) since last week.

32. 明天这个时候，我们将在飞机上。  
    This time tomorrow, we ______ (fly / will be flying / will have flown) on the plane.

## 答案

<summary>点击展开答案</summary>

1. **drink** — 一般现在时，表习惯。
2. **is writing** — 现在进行时，right now 表此刻进行。
3. **went** — 一般过去时，yesterday 表过去具体时间。
4. **have finished** — 现在完成时，已经做完，与现在有联系。
5. **will be meeting** — 将来进行时，表将来某时刻正在进行。
6. **had started** — 过去完成时，「到达」之前「已开始」。
7. **has been studying** — 现在完成进行时，since 表从过去持续到现在。
8. **was born** — 一般过去时被动，出生是过去事件。
9. **will have lived** — 将来完成时，by + 将来时间点，表到那时已完成。
10. **were playing** — 过去进行时，at that time 表过去某刻正在进行。
11. **boils** — 一般现在时，表客观真理。
12. **Have … seen** — 现在完成时，ever/before 常用于完成时。
13. **will call** — 一般将来时，next week 表将来。
14. **had written** — 过去完成时，before he left 表「离开前已完成」。
15. **is coming** — 现在进行时，Look! 表眼前正在发生。
16. **have worked** — 现在完成时，for five years + 现在还在这。
17. **was watching** — 过去进行时，at 8 pm last night 表过去某刻进行。
18. **Will … have been finished** — 将来完成时被动，by Friday 表截止将来某时。
19. **is taking** — 现在进行时，today 与 usually 对比，表今天例外。
20. **haven't seen** — 现在完成时，since 引导的时间点。
21. **will have** — 一般将来时表预测；by 2030 也可接 will have had，但此句用 will have 表将来状态即可。
22. **would come** — 间接引语中，主句过去时，从句将来改为 would come。
23. **leaves** — 一般现在时表时刻表上的固定安排；也可用 is leaving 表近期安排，但时刻表用 leaves 更自然。
24. **has been waiting** — 现在完成进行时，all morning 表从今早持续到现在。
25. **was preparing** — 过去进行时，this time last year 表过去某刻正在进行。
26. **had discussed** — 过去完成进行时也可用 had been discussing；had discussed for three hours 表到会议结束时已持续三小时。
27. **will improve** — 条件句 if 从句用一般现在时，主句用 will + 动词原形表将来结果。
28. **have just washed** — 现在完成时，just 表刚刚完成。
29. **had worked** — 过去完成时，by then 表到过去某时为止已持续十年。
30. **will help** — 一般将来时，表说话人的承诺。
31. **has been raining** — 现在完成进行时，since last week 表从过去持续到现在且可能继续。
32. **will be flying** — 将来进行时，this time tomorrow 表将来某刻正在进行。
