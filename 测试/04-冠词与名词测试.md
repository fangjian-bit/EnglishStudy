# 04 · 冠词与名词测试

> 考查 **a / an / the / 零冠词** 及 **可数 / 不可数名词**。  
> 理论 → [../底层笔记/底层冠词与名词.md](../底层笔记/底层冠词与名词.md)

**说明：** 选择正确选项，或在空格处填入 a / an / the / ×（零冠词）。

---

## 题目

1. I need ______ advice on this problem.  
   A. a  B. an  C. the  D. ×

2. She is ______ honest person.  
   A. a  B. an  C. the  D. ×

3. ______ sun rises in ______ east.  
   A. A; a  B. The; the  C. The; ×  D. ×; the

4. I like ______ music, especially ______ piano.  
   A. ×; the  B. the; ×  C. ×; ×  D. the; the

5. He gave me ______ useful information.  
   A. a  B. an  C. some  D. many

6. There is ______ university near my home.  
   A. a  B. an  C. the  D. ×

7. ______ water in this bottle is clean.  
   A. A  B. An  C. The  D. ×

8. I saw ______ elephant at ______ zoo yesterday.  
   A. an; the  B. a; a  C. the; the  D. an; ×

9. ______ children need love and care.  
   A. A  B. An  C. The  D. ×

10. Can you pass me ______ salt, please?  
    A. a  B. an  C. the  D. ×

11. She bought ______ bread and ______ apple.  
    A. ×; an  B. a; a  C. the; the  D. ×; a

12. He is ______ European, not ______ Asian.  
    A. a; an  B. an; a  C. a; a  D. an; an

13. I have two ______ and three ______ of furniture.  
    A. childs; piece  B. children; pieces  C. children; piece  D. child; pieces

14. ______ news is spreading fast.  
    A. A  B. An  C. The  D. ×

15. She works as ______ engineer in ______ company I told you about.  
    A. an; the  B. a; a  C. an; a  D. the; the

16. We had ______ fun at the party.  
    A. a  B. many  C. much  D. ×

17. ______ Mount Everest is ______ highest mountain in the world.  
    A. ×; the  B. The; the  C. ×; a  D. The; ×

18. I need to buy ______ milk, ______ eggs, and ______ rice.  
    A. ×; ×; ×  B. a; the; ×  C. the; ×; a  D. ×; the; the

19. It took me ______ hour to finish ______ homework.  
    A. a; the  B. an; the  C. an; ×  D. a; ×

20. ______ more you read, ______ better you write.  
    A. ×; ×  B. The; the  C. A; a  D. The; a

21. She gave me ______ good news and ______ piece of bad news.  
    A. a; a  B. ×; a  C. the; the  D. some; ×

22. Is this ______ same book you borrowed last week?  
    A. a  B. an  C. the  D. ×

23. I need ______ umbrella because ______ weather is terrible today.  
    A. a; the  B. an; the  C. the; ×  D. an; ×

24. She plays ______ violin and ______ basketball every weekend.  
    A. the; ×  B. ×; the  C. the; the  D. ×; ×

25. ______ rich should help ______ poor.  
    A. A; a  B. The; the  C. ×; ×  D. The; ×

26. He told me ______ interesting story about ______ history of China.  
    A. an; the  B. a; ×  C. an; ×  D. the; the

27. How many ______ do you need? I only have two ______ left.  
    A. paper; sheet  B. papers; sheets  C. pieces of paper; sheets  D. paper; sheets of

28. ______ United States is ______ large country.  
    A. ×; a  B. The; a  C. A; the  D. The; the

29. We had ______ wonderful time at ______ beach.  
    A. a; the  B. a; ×  C. the; the  D. ×; the

30. She is ______ only child in ______ family of five.  
    A. the; a  B. an; the  C. the; the  D. ×; a

31. I bought two ______ of ______ and a loaf of ______.  
    A. bottles; milk; bread  B. bottle; milks; breads  C. bottles; milks; bread  D. bottle; milk; breads

32. ______ harder you work, ______ luckier you get.  
    A. ×; ×  B. A; a  C. The; the  D. The; a

---

## 答案

<details>
<summary>点击展开答案</summary>

1. **D. ×** — advice 不可数，不能说 a advice；可说 a piece of advice。
2. **B. an** — honest 发音以元音 /ɒ/ 开头，用 an。
3. **B. The; the** — 独一无二的天体 the sun；方位 the east 表泛指方向。
4. **A. ×; the** — music 泛指用零冠词；the piano 表「钢琴这种乐器」时可特指类别。
5. **C. some** — information 不可数，用 some；many 修饰可数名词。
6. **A. a** — university 发音以辅音 /j/ 开头，用 a。
7. **C. The** — this bottle 限定，特指这瓶里的水。
8. **A. an; the** — elephant 以元音开头；at the zoo 特指某个动物园或泛指动物园类别。
9. **D. ×** — 泛指「孩子们」这一类，零冠词复数表类别。
10. **C. the** — 餐桌上双方都知道的盐，特指。
11. **A. ×; an** — bread 不可数；apple 可数单数用 an。
12. **A. a; an** — European 发 /j/ 用 a；Asian 发元音用 an。
13. **B. children; pieces** — child 不规则复数 children；furniture 不可数，用 pieces of。
14. **C. The** — 这条新闻双方已知或特指正在传播的那条。
15. **A. an; the** — engineer 以元音开头；company I told you about 是特指。
16. **D. ×** — have fun 固定搭配，fun 不可数，零冠词。
17. **A. ×; the** — 专有名词 Mount Everest 前通常零冠词；最高级前用 the。
18. **A. ×; ×; ×** — milk、eggs（复数泛指）、rice（不可数）均不需冠词表一般购买。
19. **B. an; the** — hour 中 h 不发音；the homework 特指某次作业。
20. **B. The; the** — the more … the better … 固定比较结构。
21. **B. ×; a** — news 不可数零冠词；a piece of 表「一条」坏消息。
22. **C. the** — the same 固定搭配。
23. **B. an; the** — umbrella 以元音开头用 an；the weather 特指今天的天气。
24. **A. the; ×** — 西洋乐器前用 the（play the violin）；球类运动零冠词（play basketball）。
25. **B. The; the** — the + 形容词表一类人：the rich（富人）、the poor（穷人）。
26. **A. an; the** — interesting 以元音开头；the history of China 特指中国历史。
27. **C. pieces of paper; sheets** — paper 不可数，用 pieces of paper；sheet 可数，two sheets。
28. **B. The; a** — 国名含普通名词（States）用 the；large country 泛指用 a。
29. **A. a; the** — have a wonderful time 固定搭配；the beach 特指双方知道的那片海滩。
30. **A. the; a** — the only child 唯一的孩子；a family of five 一个五口之家。
31. **A. bottles; milk; bread** — two bottles of milk（两瓶牛奶）；milk、bread 不可数；a loaf of bread 固定搭配。
32. **C. The; the** — The harder …, the luckier … 与 the more … the better 同类比较结构。

</details>
