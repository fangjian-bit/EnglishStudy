# 08 · 综合测试

## 题目

**一、句子成分（1–5）**

1. 指出 **in the morning** 在句 "I jog in the morning." 中的成分。  
   A. 主语  B. 谓语  C. 宾语  D. 状语

2. 指出 **interesting** 在句 "This is an interesting story." 中的成分。  
   A. 表语  B. 定语  C. 状语  D. 宾补

3. "She became a singer." 中 **a singer** 是：  
   A. 宾语  B. 表语  C. 定语  D. 状语

4. "We found the task difficult." 中 **difficult** 是：  
   A. 表语  B. 定语  C. 宾补  D. 状语

5. "Reading books enriches the mind." 中 **Reading books** 是：  
   A. 主语  B. 谓语  C. 宾语  D. 状语

**二、词类（6–10）**

6. **slowly** 的词类是：  
   A. 形容词  B. 副词  C. 介词  D. 连词

7. **between** 的词类是：  
   A. 副词  B. 介词  C. 连词  D. 动词

8. **whose** 在 "Whose bag is this?" 中的词类是：  
   A. 副词  B. 疑问代词/限定词  C. 连词  D. 介词

9. **happily** 修饰的是：  
   "The children played happily in the yard."  
   A. children  B. played  C. yard  D. the

10. **and** 在 "Tea and coffee are both popular." 中的词类是：  
    A. 介词  B. 连词  C. 冠词  D. 副词

**三、时态（11–16）**

11. I ______ (read) this novel twice. It's great.  
    A. read  B. have read  C. am reading  D. was reading

12. While I ______ (cook), the phone rang.  
    A. cooked  B. was cooking  C. have cooked  D. cook

13. By next month, she ______ (work) here for a year.  
    A. works  B. will work  C. will have worked  D. has worked

14. He ______ (not finish) his lunch yet.  
    A. didn't finish  B. hasn't finished  C. doesn't finish  D. wasn't finishing

15. Look at those clouds! It ______ (rain).  
    A. rains  B. is going to rain  C. rained  D. has rained

16. When I was young, I ______ (play) outside every day.  
    A. play  B. played  C. have played  D. am playing

**四、冠词与名词（17–21）**

17. She gave me ______ useful suggestion.  
    A. a  B. an  C. the  D. ×

18. ______ information you gave me was very helpful.  
    A. A  B. An  C. The  D. ×

19. I need to buy ______ furniture for my new apartment.  
    A. a  B. an  C. some  D. many

20. He is ______ tallest boy in our class.  
    A. a  B. an  C. the  D. ×

21. 判断正误：She has many homeworks to do. ✓ / ✗

**五、宾补与不定式（22–25）**

22. My father doesn't allow me ______ out after 10 pm.  
    A. go  B. to go  C. going  D. went

23. The coach made the team ______ harder.  
    A. to train  B. train  C. training  D. trained

24. I heard her ______ a song in the next room.（正在唱）  
    A. sing  B. to sing  C. singing  D. sung

25. We had the roof ______ after the storm.  
    A. repair  B. to repair  C. repaired  D. repairing

**六、翻译（26–30）**

26. 译成英文：他三点钟会来。

27. 译成英文：这个问题太难了，我理解不了。

28. 译成中文：I have been waiting for you for half an hour.

29. 译成中文：She suggested that we should leave early.

30. 译成英文：请别忘了明天带伞。

**七、从句（31–35）**

31. "I don't know **where he lives**." 中划线部分是：  
    A. 定语从句  B. 宾语从句  C. 状语从句  D. 主语从句

32. The reason **why he was late** is still unknown. 中 why he was late 是：  
    A. 定语从句  B. 同位语从句  C. 宾语从句  D. 状语从句

33. **If it rains tomorrow**, we will cancel the picnic. 中 If 引导的是：  
    A. 条件状语从句  B. 时间状语从句  C. 定语从句  D. 宾语从句

34. 选择：This is the house ______ I was born.  
    A. which  B. where  C. that  D. who

35. 选择：She asked me ______ I had finished the report.  
    A. that  B. if  C. what  D. which

**八、短语动词（36–40）**

36. Please **turn off** the lights when you leave. turn off 的意思是：  
    A. 打开  B. 关掉  C. 调大  D. 转向

37. I need to **look up** this word in the dictionary. look up 此处意为：  
    A. 向上看  B. 尊敬  C. 查阅  D. 拜访

38. 选择：Don't give ______! Keep trying.  
    A. up  B. in  C. out  D. away

39. 选择：She **came across** an old photo while cleaning the attic.  
    came across 意为：  
    A. 穿过  B. 偶然发现  C. 走过来  D. 反对

40. 选择：We have to **put off** the meeting until next week.  
    A. 推迟  B. 穿上  C. 放下  D. 扑灭

**九、固定搭配（41–45）**

41. 选择：I'm interested ______ learning French.  
    A. on  B. in  C. at  D. for

42. 选择：She is afraid ______ spiders.  
    A. of  B. to  C. for  D. about

43. 选择：He apologized ______ being late.  
    A. for  B. to  C. about  D. of

44. 选择：This book is different ______ that one.  
    A. to  B. from  C. with  D. than

45. 选择：I'm looking forward ______ your reply.  
    A. to  B. for  C. at  D. ×

**十、虚拟语气与间接引语（46–50）**

46. If I ______ you, I would accept the offer.  
    A. am  B. was  C. were  D. be

47. I wish I ______ harder when I was in school.  
    A. studied  B. had studied  C. study  D. would study

48. He said, "I am tired." → 间接引语：He said that he ______ tired.  
    A. is  B. was  C. has been  D. had been

49. She asked, "Where do you live?" → 间接引语：She asked where I ______.  
    A. live  B. lived  C. am living  D. have lived

50. The teacher suggested that we ______ more exercises.  
    A. do  B. did  C. will do  D. doing

## 答案

<details>
<summary>点击展开答案</summary>

1. **D. 状语** — in the morning 表时间，修饰 jog。
2. **B. 定语** — interesting 修饰 story。
3. **B. 表语** — become 是系动词，a singer 描述主语。
4. **C. 宾补** — find + 宾语 + 宾补，difficult 补充说明 task。
5. **A. 主语** — 动名词短语 Reading books 作主语。

6. **B. 副词** — 修饰动词，表方式。
7. **B. 介词** — between 后接名词，表「在……之间」。
8. **B. 疑问代词/限定词** — 询问所属关系。
9. **B. played** — 副词 happily 修饰动词 played。
10. **B. 连词** — 连接 tea 和 coffee 两个并列主语。

11. **B. have read** — twice 暗示经历，用现在完成时。
12. **B. was cooking** — while 引导背景动作，用过去进行时；rang 为一般过去时。
13. **C. will have worked** — by next month 表将来截止点，用将来完成时。
14. **B. hasn't finished** — yet 与现在完成时连用。
15. **B. is going to rain** — Look at those clouds 表根据迹象预测。
16. **B. played** — when I was young 表过去习惯。

17. **A. a** — suggestion 可数；useful 发 /j/ 开头，用 a。
18. **C. The** — you gave me 限定，特指那条信息。
19. **C. some** — furniture 不可数，用 some。
20. **C. the** — 最高级 tallest 前用 the。
21. **✗** — homework 不可数，应为 much homework 或 a lot of homework。

22. **B. to go** — allow + 宾语 + to do。
23. **B. train** — make + 宾语 + 裸不定式。
24. **C. singing** — hear + 宾语 + doing 表听见正在进行。
25. **C. repaired** — have + 物 + 过去分词，请别人修理。

26. **He will come at three o'clock.** / **He is coming at three o'clock.**  
    一般将来时或现在进行表将来均可。

27. **The question is too difficult for me to understand.**  
    too … to … 结构表「太……而不能……」。

28. **我已经等了你半个小时了。**  
    现在完成进行时 have been waiting 表从过去持续到现在的动作。

29. **她建议我们应该早点离开。**  
    suggest that + 从句（should 可省略）；leave early 早点离开。

30. **Please don't forget to bring an umbrella tomorrow.**  
    forget to do 忘记去做；bring an umbrella 带伞。

31. **B. 宾语从句** — know 的宾语是 where he lives，作 know 的对象。
32. **A. 定语从句** — why he was late 修饰 reason，说明原因的内容。
33. **A. 条件状语从句** — If 引导条件，表「如果下雨」。
34. **B. where** — 先行词 house 表地点，从句缺地点状语，用 where。
35. **B. if** — ask 后接 whether/if 引导的宾语从句，表「是否」。

36. **B. 关掉** — turn off 关闭（电器、灯等）。
37. **C. 查阅** — look up a word 在词典中查单词。
38. **A. up** — give up 放弃，固定短语动词。
39. **B. 偶然发现** — come across 偶然遇见/发现。
40. **A. 推迟** — put off 推迟、延期。

41. **B. in** — be interested in + 动名词/名词，对……感兴趣。
42. **A. of** — be afraid of 害怕……。
43. **A. for** — apologize for + 动名词/名词，为……道歉。
44. **B. from** — be different from 与……不同（美式）；英式也可用 different to。
45. **A. to** — look forward to + 动名词，to 是介词不是不定式符号。

46. **C. were** — 与现在事实相反的虚拟语气，If I were you 固定表达。
47. **B. had studied** — wish 后接与过去事实相反的虚拟，用过去完成时 had studied。
48. **B. was** — 直接引语 am → 间接引语 was（主句 said 为过去时）。
49. **B. lived** — 直接引语 do you live（一般现在时）→ 间接引语 lived（时态后移）；语序改为陈述语序 where I lived。
50. **A. do** — suggest that + (should) + 动词原形，表建议，用 do。
