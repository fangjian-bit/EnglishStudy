# 05 · 宾补与不定式测试

> 考查 **to do vs 裸不定式**、**allow vs let**、感官动词宾补等。  
> 理论 → [../底层笔记/底层使役与特殊句式.md](../底层笔记/底层使役与特殊句式.md)

**说明：** 选择正确形式填空，或判断句子正误。

---

## 题目

1. My mother let me ______ (to go / go) out last night.

2. The teacher made us ______ (to stay / stay) after class.

3. She allowed her son ______ (play / to play) video games on weekends.

4. I saw him ______ (cross / to cross / crossing) the road just now.（看见全过程）

5. We heard someone ______ (knock / to knock / knocking) at the door.（正在敲）

6. They got the mechanic ______ (repair / to repair / repaired) the car.

7. I'll have my assistant ______ (call / to call) you back.

8. The story made me ______ (cry / to cry).

9. Please let ______ (I / me) ______ (try / to try) again.

10. 判断正误：He allowed me to go. ✓ / ✗

11. 判断正误：He allowed me go. ✓ / ✗

12. 判断正误：Let me to help you. ✓ / ✗

13. 选择：My parents don't allow me ______ late.  
    A. stay up  B. to stay up  C. staying up  D. stayed up

14. 选择：I want you ______ this letter.  
    A. mail  B. to mail  C. mailing  D. mailed

15. 选择：We had our house ______ last month.  
    A. paint  B. to paint  C. painted  D. painting

16. 填空：The boss had his secretary ______ (type) the report.（安排秘书做）

17. 填空：I felt the ground ______ (shake) during the earthquake.

18. 选择：She watched her daughter ______ the piano.（看见正在弹）  
    A. play  B. to play  C. playing  D. played

19. 选择：What made you ______ so angry?  
    A. feel  B. to feel  C. feeling  D. felt

20. 填空：They couldn't get him ______ (agree) with the plan.

21. 判断正误：Make him to apologize. ✓ / ✗

22. 选择：The law requires every citizen ______ taxes.  
    A. pay  B. to pay  C. paying  D. paid

23. 填空：Don't let the children ______ (run) in the hallway.

24. 选择：I noticed him ______ the door quietly.（看见正在关）  
    A. close  B. to close  C. closing  D. closed

25. 填空：The manager had the documents ______ (translate) into English.

26. 判断正误：She lets her dog to sleep on the sofa. ✓ / ✗

27. 选择：What caused you ______ so much?  
    A. worry  B. to worry  C. worrying  D. worried

28. 填空：We watched the sun ______ (set) behind the mountains.

29. 选择：My parents won't allow me ______ TV on weekdays.  
    A. watch  B. to watch  C. watching  D. watched

30. 填空：The teacher got all the students ______ (stand) up when the principal entered.

31. 选择：I felt something ______ up my leg.（感到有东西在爬）  
    A. crawl  B. to crawl  C. crawling  D. crawled

32. 判断正误：Help me carrying these boxes. ✓ / ✗

---

## 答案

<details>
<summary>点击展开答案</summary>

1. **go** — let + 宾语 + **动词原形**（裸不定式），不加 to。
2. **stay** — make + 宾语 + **动词原形**。
3. **to play** — allow + 宾语 + **to do**。
4. **cross** — see + 宾语 + **do** 表看见全过程；crossing 表正在进行。
5. **knocking** — hear + 宾语 + **doing** 表听见正在进行的动作。
6. **repair / to repair** — get + 宾语 + **to do** 表「让某人做」；get + 物 + **done** 表「让某物被做」。此处 mechanic 是人，用 to repair；若选 repaired 则宾语应为 the car（get the car repaired）。
7. **call** — have + 宾语（人）+ **动词原形** 表安排某人做。
8. **cry** — make + 宾语 + **动词原形**。
9. **me; try** — let + **宾格代词** + **动词原形**。
10. **✓** — allow 后接 to do，正确。
11. **✗** — allow 后不能接裸不定式，应为 allowed me to go。
12. **✗** — let 后接动词原形，不能加 to：Let me help you.
13. **B. to stay up** — allow + 宾语 + to do。
14. **B. to mail** — want + 宾语 + to do。
15. **C. painted** — have + 物 + **过去分词** 表请别人做（被动完成）。
16. **type** — have + 人 + **动词原形** 表安排某人做。
17. **shake** — feel + 宾语 + **do** 表感觉到完整动作；shaking 表感到正在震动。
18. **C. playing** — watch + 宾语 + **doing** 表看见正在进行。
19. **A. feel** — make + 宾语 + **动词原形**（主动语态）；被动则为 was made to feel。
20. **to agree** — get + 宾语 + **to do** 表「使某人做某事」。
21. **✗** — make 主动语态后接裸不定式：Make him apologize.
22. **B. to pay** — require + 宾语 + **to do**。
23. **run** — let + 宾语 + **动词原形**，不加 to。
24. **C. closing** — notice + 宾语 + **doing** 表看见正在进行的动作。
25. **translated** — have + 物 + **过去分词** 表请人把文件翻译好（被动完成）。
26. **✗** — let 后接裸不定式：She lets her dog sleep on the sofa.
27. **B. to worry** — cause + 宾语 + **to do** 表「使某人做/处于某种状态」。
28. **set** — watch + 宾语 + **do** 表看见全过程（日落整个过程）；也可用 setting 强调进行。
29. **B. to watch** — allow + 宾语 + **to do**。
30. **stand** — get + 宾语 + **to do** 或 **do**（美式口语中 get sb to do 更常见）；get all the students to stand / stand up。
31. **C. crawling** — feel + 宾语 + **doing** 表感觉到正在进行的动作。
32. **✗** — help 后可接 to do 或 do，也可接动名词，但 help me carrying 不对；应为 Help me carry / to carry these boxes。

</details>
