# 13 · 间接引语测试

> 考查 **直接引语变间接引语**：时态后移、人称变化、时间/地点词转换。  
> 理论 → [../底层笔记/底层间接引语.md](../底层笔记/底层间接引语.md)

**说明：** 将直接引语改为间接引语，或选择正确形式。先独立完成，再展开答案。

---

## 题目

1. He said, "I am busy." → He said that he ______ busy.  
   A. is  B. was  C. has been  D. had been

2. She said, "I will call you tomorrow." → She said that she ______ me the next day.  
   A. will call  B. would call  C. calls  D. called

3. Tom said, "I have finished my homework." → Tom said that he ______ his homework.  
   A. has finished  B. had finished  C. finished  D. finishes

4. She asked, "Are you coming to the party?" → She asked if I ______ to the party.  
   A. am coming  B. was coming  C. come  D. came

5. He said, "I can swim." → He said that he ______ swim.  
   A. can  B. could  C. will  D. may

6. Mary said, "I went to Paris last year." → Mary said that she ______ to Paris ______.  
   A. went; last year  B. had gone; the year before  C. has gone; last year  D. went; the year before

7. He said, "I am reading a novel now." → He said that he ______ a novel ______.  
   A. is reading; now  B. was reading; then  C. read; then  D. was reading; now

8. She asked, "Where do you live?" → She asked where I ______.  
   A. live  B. lived  C. am living  D. living

9. He said, "Don't open the window." → He told me ______ the window.  
   A. don't open  B. not open  C. not to open  D. to not open

10. She said, "I must leave now." → She said that she ______ leave ______.  
    A. must; now  B. had to; then  C. must; then  D. had to; now

11. He asked, "What time is it?" → He asked what time ______.  
    A. it is  B. it was  C. is it  D. was it

12. She said, "I was watching TV at 8 pm yesterday." → She said that she ______ TV at 8 pm ______.  
    A. was watching; the day before  B. had been watching; the day before  C. watched; yesterday  D. was watching; yesterday

13. He said, "I may be late." → He said that he ______ be late.  
    A. may  B. might  C. can  D. will

14. She asked, "Have you seen this film?" → She asked if I ______ that film.  
    A. have seen  B. had seen  C. saw  D. see

15. He said, "I will be studying at this time tomorrow." → He said that he ______ studying at that time the next day.  
    A. will be  B. would be  C. is  D. was

16. She said, "I have been waiting for an hour." → She said that she ______ for an hour.  
    A. has been waiting  B. had been waiting  C. was waiting  D. waited

17. He asked, "Who broke the vase?" → He asked who ______ the vase.  
    A. breaks  B. broke  C. had broken  D. has broken

18. She said, "Please help me." → She asked me ______ her.  
    A. help  B. to help  C. helping  D. helped

19. He said, "I shall return in a week." → He said that he ______ return in ______.  
    A. shall; a week  B. would; a week  C. should; a week  D. would; the following week

20. She asked, "Why are you crying?" → She asked why I ______.  
    A. am crying  B. was crying  C. cry  D. cried

21. He said, "This book is mine." → He said that ______ book was ______.  
    A. this; mine  B. that; his  C. the; mine  D. that; mine

22. She said, "I saw him here yesterday." → She said that she ______ him ______ ______.  
    A. saw; here; yesterday  B. had seen; there; the day before  C. saw; there; the day before  D. had seen; here; yesterday

23. He asked, "Will you marry me?" → He asked if I ______ marry him.  
    A. will  B. would  C. shall  D. should

24. She said, "I should study harder." → She said that she ______ study harder.  
    A. should  B. would  C. must  D. had to

25. 选择正确的间接引语：  
    直接引语："I don't like coffee," she said.  
    A. She said she doesn't like coffee.  
    B. She said she didn't like coffee.  
    C. She said she hadn't liked coffee.  
    D. She said she won't like coffee.

---

## 答案

<details>
<summary>点击展开答案</summary>

1. **B. was** — am → was（时态后移）。
2. **B. would call** — will call → would call；tomorrow → the next day。
3. **B. had finished** — have finished → had finished（现在完成时 → 过去完成时）。
4. **B. was coming** — Are you coming → was coming；一般现在进行 → 过去进行。
5. **B. could** — can → could。
6. **B. had gone; the year before** — went → had gone（若强调先于 said 则用过去完成时）；last year → the year before。
7. **B. was reading; then** — am reading → was reading；now → then。
8. **B. lived** — do you live → lived；疑问语序改为陈述语序 where I lived。
9. **C. not to open** — 否定祈使 Don't open → told sb not to open。
10. **B. had to; then** — must → had to（或 must 保留）；now → then。
11. **B. it was** — is → was；宾语从句用陈述语序 what time it was。
12. **A. was watching; the day before** — was watching 可保留或改为 had been watching；yesterday → the day before。
13. **B. might** — may → might。
14. **B. had seen** — Have you seen → had seen。
15. **B. would be** — will be → would be studying（将来进行 → 过去将来进行）。
16. **B. had been waiting** — have been waiting → had been waiting。
17. **C. had broken** — broke 若发生在 asked 之前，用 had broken；若与 said 同时可知用 broke 也可，此处选 had broken 更严谨。
18. **B. to help** — Please help me → asked sb to help。
19. **D. would; the following week** — shall/will → would；in a week 可保留，the following week 对应将来时间后移。
20. **B. was crying** — are crying → was crying。
21. **B. that; his** — this → that；mine → his（人称随主语 he 变化）。
22. **B. had seen; there; the day before** — here → there；yesterday → the day before；saw → saw/had seen。
23. **B. would** — Will you → would；me → him。
24. **A. should** — should 表建议可保留，不必然改为 would。
25. **B. She said she didn't like coffee.** — don't like → didn't like，时态后移。

</details>
