# 12 · 虚拟语气测试

> 考查 **与现在/过去/将来事实相反** 的虚拟语气及 **wish、suggest** 等用法。  
> 理论 → [../底层笔记/底层虚拟语气.md](../底层笔记/底层虚拟语气.md)

**说明：** 选择正确动词形式。先独立完成，再展开答案。

---

## 题目

1. If I ______ rich, I would travel the world.  
   A. am  B. was  C. were  D. be

2. If she ______ harder, she would have passed the exam.  
   A. studied  B. had studied  C. studies  D. would study

3. If it ______ tomorrow, the sports day will be postponed.  
   A. rains  B. will rain  C. rained  D. would rain

4. I wish I ______ speak French fluently.  
   A. can  B. could  C. will  D. would

5. I wish I ______ that movie last night.  
   A. didn't watch  B. hadn't watched  C. don't watch  D. wouldn't watch

6. He talks as if he ______ everything.  
   A. knows  B. knew  C. has known  D. had known

7. It's time we ______ home.  
   A. go  B. went  C. will go  D. have gone

8. I'd rather you ______ here tomorrow.  
   A. come  B. came  C. will come  D. have come

9. The doctor suggested that he ______ smoking.  
   A. quit  B. quits  C. quit / should quit  D. quitting

10. It is essential that every student ______ on time.  
    A. is  B. be  C. was  D. will be

11. If only I ______ more attention in class!  
    A. paid  B. pay  C. had paid  D. would pay

12. Suppose you ______ a million dollars, what would you do?  
    A. win  B. won  C. had won  D. will win

13. Without your help, I ______ the project.  
    A. can't finish  B. couldn't finish  C. couldn't have finished  D. won't finish

14. If he ______ the bus, he wouldn't have been late.  
    A. caught  B. had caught  C. catches  D. would catch

15. She wishes she ______ taller.  
    A. is  B. was  C. were  D. be

16. I would have called you if I ______ your number.  
    A. knew  B. know  C. had known  D. have known

17. It's high time the government ______ action on climate change.  
    A. take  B. took  C. takes  D. will take

18. The boss insisted that the report ______ submitted by Friday.  
    A. is  B. be  C. was  D. will be

19. If you ______ me earlier, I could have helped you.  
    A. told  B. tell  C. had told  D. would tell

20. I'd rather he ______ the truth now.  
    A. tells  B. told  C. tell  D. will tell

21. 选择正确句子：  
    A. If I was you, I would go.  
    B. If I were you, I would go.  
    C. If I am you, I will go.  
    D. If I were you, I will go.

22. But for the rain, we ______ the picnic.  
    A. would have  B. would have had  C. will have  D. had

23. He acts as though nothing ______.  
    A. happens  B. happened  C. has happened  D. had happened

24. It is strange that he ______ so angry.  
    A. is  B. be  C. was  D. were

25. If only she ______ here with us now!  
    A. is  B. was  C. were  D. be

---

## 答案

<details>
<summary>点击展开答案</summary>

1. **C. were** — 与现在事实相反，If I were rich（正式语法中所有人称用 were）。
2. **B. had studied** — 与过去事实相反，If + 过去完成时，主句 would have + 过去分词。
3. **A. rains** — 真实条件句（有可能发生），if 从句用一般现在时表将来。
4. **B. could** — wish + 与现在事实相反，用过去式 could 表「能」。
5. **B. hadn't watched** — wish + 与过去事实相反，用过去完成时。
6. **B. knew** — as if 好象，与现在事实可能相反，用过去式 knew。
7. **B. went** — It's time (that) + 过去式，表「该……的时候了」。
8. **B. came** — would rather + 从句（指他人），用过去式表虚拟。
9. **C. quit / should quit** — suggest that + (should) + 动词原形，表建议。
10. **B. be** — It is essential/vital/important that + (should) + 动词原形。
11. **C. had paid** — If only 要是……就好了；与过去遗憾，用过去完成时。
12. **B. won** — Suppose 假设，与现在/将来虚拟，从句用过去式 won。
13. **C. couldn't have finished** — Without = If it hadn't been for，与过去相反，主句 would/could have + 过去分词。
14. **B. had caught** — 与过去事实相反，If he had caught the bus …
15. **C. were** — wish 与现在愿望相反，she were taller。
16. **C. had known** — 与过去相反，If I had known your number, I would have called。
17. **B. took** — It's high time + 过去式，表紧迫建议。
18. **B. be** — insist that + (should) + 动词原形，表坚决要求。
19. **C. had told** — 与过去相反，If you had told me earlier …
20. **B. told** — would rather he told 宁愿他现在说实话（与现在/将来虚拟）。
21. **B. If I were you, I would go.** — 虚拟语气标准形式：were + would。
22. **B. would have had** — But for = 要不是，与过去相反：would have had the picnic。
23. **B. happened** — as though 与现在情况不符，用过去式 happened。
24. **B. be** — It is strange/odd that + 动词原形/should，表惊讶（虚拟语气）。
25. **C. were** — If only 与现在愿望，she were here。

</details>
