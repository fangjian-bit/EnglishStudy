## 题目

1. She is good ______ playing the piano.  
   A. at  B. in  C. on  D. for

2. I'm tired ______ waiting for the bus.  
   A. of  B. from  C. with  D. about

3. He succeeded ______ passing the exam.  
   A. to  B. in  C. on  D. at

4. Thank you ______ your help.  
   A. for  B. to  C. about  D. of

5. She is famous ______ her novels.  
   A. for  B. as  C. with  D. about

6. I'm familiar ______ this area.  
   A. to  B. with  C. about  D. for

7. He is responsible ______ the project.  
   A. of  B. for  C. to  D. with

8. She complained ______ the poor service.  
   A. about  B. for  C. to  D. of

9. I'm proud ______ my students.  
   A. of  B. for  C. about  D. with

10. He insisted ______ paying the bill.  
    A. on  B. in  C. to  D. for

11. She apologized ______ the delay.  
    A. for  B. to  C. about  D. of

12. I'm worried ______ his health.  
    A. about  B. for  C. of  D. with

13. He is allergic ______ peanuts.  
    A. to  B. of  C. for  D. with

14. She is married ______ a doctor.  
    A. with  B. to  C. for  D. by

15. I'm satisfied ______ the result.  
    A. with  B. of  C. for  D. about

16. He is capable ______ solving complex problems.  
    A. of  B. to  C. for  D. in

17. She is aware ______ the risks involved.  
    A. of  B. about  C. for  D. to

18. I'm grateful ______ your kindness.  
    A. for  B. to  C. about  D. of

19. He is addicted ______ video games.  
    A. to  B. on  C. with  D. for

20. She is qualified ______ the position.  
    A. for  B. to  C. as  D. in

21. I'm confident ______ our team's ability.  
    A. in  B. of  C. about  D. for

22. He is jealous ______ his brother's success.  
    A. of  B. about  C. for  D. with

23. She is fond ______ classical music.  
    A. of  B. in  C. with  D. about

24. I'm short ______ cash at the moment.  
    A. of  B. in  C. on  D. for

25. He was accused ______ stealing company data.  
    A. of  B. for  C. with  D. about

## 答案

<details>
<summary>点击展开答案</summary>

1. **A. at** — be good at + 动名词/名词，擅长……。
2. **A. of** — be tired of 厌倦、厌烦……。
3. **B. in** — succeed in doing 成功做某事。
4. **A. for** — thank sb for sth 因某事感谢某人。
5. **A. for** — be famous for 因……而著名；be famous as 作为……而著名。
6. **B. with** — be familiar with 熟悉……；be familiar to 为……所熟悉。
7. **B. for** — be responsible for 对……负责；be responsible to 向……负责。
8. **A. about** — complain about 抱怨……；complain to sb 向某人投诉。
9. **A. of** — be proud of 为……感到骄傲。
10. **A. on** — insist on + 动名词，坚持……。
11. **A. for** — apologize for 为……道歉；apologize to sb 向某人道歉。
12. **A. about** — be worried about 担心……。
13. **A. to** — be allergic to 对……过敏。
14. **B. to** — be married to sb 与某人结婚。
15. **A. with** — be satisfied with 对……满意。
16. **A. of** — be capable of doing 有能力做……。
17. **A. of** — be aware of 意识到、知道……。
18. **A. for** — be grateful for 因……感激；be grateful to sb 感激某人。
19. **A. to** — be addicted to 对……上瘾。
20. **A. for** — be qualified for 有资格胜任……。
21. **A. in** — be confident in 对……有信心。
22. **A. of** — be jealous of 嫉妒……。
23. **A. of** — be fond of 喜欢……。
24. **A. of** — be short of 缺乏……。
25. **A. of** — be accused of 被指控……。
